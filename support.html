About Family Star

Family Star is a parent-operated kids rewards app for managing routines, tasks, points, and rewards at home.

  • Create daily or one-time tasks
  • Assign tasks to one or multiple children
  • Record task completions and point changes
  • Add or deduct points manually when needed
  • Manage a rewards catalogue and record redemptions
  • View a history log of task completions and point changes

Privacy & Data

Family Star stores all app data locally on your device.

  • No user accounts
  • No cloud storage
  • No data sharing

If you uninstall the app, locally stored data may be removed by your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do children need an account?
A: No. Family Star is designed for parents to manage routines and rewards.

Q: Can I use this offline?
A: Yes. The app can be used without an internet connection.

Q: How do I restore my data if I change phones?
A: The app stores data on-device. If your device backup restores app data, it may restore your Family Star data as well.

Q: I found a bug. What should I send?
A: Please email support with: device model, OS version, app version, and steps to reproduce.
