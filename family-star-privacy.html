Family Star – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Family Star (the "App") handles your information when you use the App. Your privacy is important to us, and we aim to be transparent and minimal in our data practices.

1. Overview

Family Star is designed to operate primarily on your device. We do not require user accounts and do not collect personal information unless explicitly stated below.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

The App allows you to create content such as:

  • Child profiles
  • Tasks, routines, and rewards
  • Points and history records

This information is stored locally on your device.

We may request access to your device’s camera or photo library to allow you to customize profiles or add images within the App. Any images you select or capture remain stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect limited, non-personal information for operational purposes, such as:

  • App version
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Anonymous diagnostics or crash reports

This information is used solely to improve app stability and performance.

2.3 Advertising Data

The free version of the App may display advertisements. Advertising partners may collect limited information such as device identifiers or interaction data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Family Star does not have access to or control over data collected by third-party advertising providers.

3. How We Use Information

We use information collected to:

  • Provide and operate the App’s features
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Display advertisements in supported versions

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Data Storage and Security

All primary app data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your data on our servers. You are responsible for safeguarding your data, including managing device security and backups where available.

5. Backup and Export

If available, backup or export features allow you to create copies of your data. These backups are stored wherever you choose (e.g., your device storage or personal cloud services). Family Star does not control or access exported data once it leaves the App.

6. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App will permanently remove locally stored data unless you have created a backup.

7. Children’s Privacy

Family Star is intended for use by parents or legal guardians. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Any child-related information entered into the App is provided and managed by an adult user and remains stored locally on the device.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services for:

  • Advertising
  • Analytics or crash reporting

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review those policies separately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
